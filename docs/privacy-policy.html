Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

QuoteSpace ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • App Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app experience.
  • Favorites: Quotes you mark as favorites are stored locally on your device.
  • Settings: Your app preferences (language, notifications) are stored locally.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Improve user experience
  • Send you daily quote notifications (if enabled)
  • Process in-app purchases

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization.
  • OpenAI API: For translation services. Text content is sent to OpenAI for translation.
  • App Store / Google Play: For processing in-app purchases.

Data Storage

All user data (favorites, settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal information on our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
