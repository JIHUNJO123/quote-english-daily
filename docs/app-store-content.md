## Description (4000 characters max)

QuoteSpace is your daily companion for inspiration, wisdom, and motivation. Discover thousands of carefully curated quotes from renowned authors, philosophers, and leaders, all automatically translated into your native language.

**Key Features:**

**Extensive Quote Collection**
Access over 2,000 commercial-use quotes from public domain sources and famous personalities. Each quote is carefully selected to provide genuine inspiration and wisdom.

**10 Inspiring Categories**
Explore quotes organized into meaningful categories: Happiness, Inspiration, Love, Success, Truth, Poetry, Life & Death, Romance, Science, and Time. Find exactly what you need to lift your spirits or guide your day.

**Automatic Translation**
Our advanced AI translation system automatically translates quotes into your language. Support for 30+ languages ensures you can enjoy wisdom in the language you understand best. Translations are cached for fast, offline access.

**Daily Notifications**
Start each day with inspiration. Set up daily notifications to receive a new quote every morning at your preferred time. Choose to receive quotes in English or your local language.

**Save Your Favorites**
Bookmark quotes that resonate with you. Your favorites are saved locally on your device, allowing you to revisit meaningful quotes anytime.

**Beautiful, Intuitive Design**
Enjoy a clean, elegant interface designed for a delightful reading experience. The app adapts to your device's theme, supporting both light and dark modes.

**Rewarded Ads**
Watch ads on your own terms to unlock free quotes. You choose when to watch ads and receive 10 free quotes for each ad viewed. This user-friendly approach respects your time while supporting the app.

**Premium Experience**
Upgrade to premium to remove all ads and enjoy unlimited access to all features. Premium is a one-time purchase that unlocks the full QuoteSpace experience.

**Privacy-Focused**
Your data stays on your device. We don't collect personal information or store your favorites on our servers. All translations and preferences are processed with your privacy in mind.
