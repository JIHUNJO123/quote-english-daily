Support Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the app language?

Go to Settings > Language > App Language, then select your preferred language from the list.

How do translations work?

Quotes are automatically translated to your selected language using AI. Translations are cached for faster loading.

How do I get free quotes?

Watch a rewarded ad to receive 10 free quotes. Tap the "Watch Ad for 10 Free Quotes" button on the home screen.

How do I remove ads?

Go to Settings > Premium > Remove Ads to purchase the premium version for $1.99.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings > Premium > Restore Purchases to restore your premium status on a new device.

How do I set up daily notifications?

Go to Settings > Notifications and enable "Daily Quote Notification". You can set the time and choose between English or your local language.

Where are my favorites stored?

All favorites are stored locally on your device. They are not synced to the cloud.

Troubleshooting

Translations are not working

Make sure you have an active internet connection. Translations require internet access for the first time.

Ads are not showing

If you're a premium user, ads are disabled. If you're not premium, check your internet connection and try again.

App crashes or freezes

Try restarting the app. If the problem persists, uninstall and reinstall the app. Your favorites and settings will be reset.
